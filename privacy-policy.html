Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the 90-Day GLP-1 Journal mobile application (the "App"), developed and operated by Istvan Balazsa ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

1. Information We Collect

The App is designed to function primarily on-device. The following categories of data may be entered by you and stored locally on your device:

  • Personal identifiers: Name, date of birth, email address (optional, entered by the user in the profile section)
  • Health and medical information: Body weight, body measurements, medication doses, injection sites, symptom ratings, blood pressure, lab values, energy levels, mood ratings, sleep quality, and other health-related notes voluntarily entered by the user
  • Nutrition data: Meal descriptions, protein intake, water consumption, daily steps, exercise logs
  • Goals and reflections: Weight goals, lifestyle goals, journal entries, mindset notes
  • Provider information: Provider name, clinic, appointment dates, and medical notes entered by the user

We do not automatically collect any of this data. All health information is entered voluntarily by you.

2. How We Use Your Information

All data you enter is used solely to provide the App's core functionality — allowing you to track your GLP-1 medication journey. Specifically:

  • To display your daily tracking history, progress charts, and weekly/monthly summaries within the App
  • To calculate totals such as protein consumed, days completed, and weight lost
  • To provide personalized reminders and goal progress indicators

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the App's features to you.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data you enter is stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage. We do not transmit your personal or health data to external servers unless you explicitly use a cloud backup feature (if available). We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information against unauthorized access.

You are responsible for securing access to your device. We recommend using device-level security (PIN, biometrics) to protect your data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data brokers.

The only circumstances under which data may be shared are:

  • With your explicit consent: If you choose to export or share your data (e.g., sharing a PDF with your healthcare provider), this is done entirely at your direction
  • Legal compliance: If required by applicable law, regulation, or valid legal process

5. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional premium subscriptions and a lifetime purchase option processed through the Google Play Store. All payment transactions are handled exclusively by Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment card details. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information about payment processing.

6. Health Data — Special Category

Health and medical data is a special category of personal information. We treat all health data you enter with the highest level of care. This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is never shared with advertisers, data brokers, or any third parties
  • Is used only to power the features you see in the App
  • Can be deleted at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App

The App is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider regarding your medication and health decisions.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • Deletion: The right to delete your data (you can delete all data by clearing the App's storage or uninstalling it)
  • Portability: The right to export your data in a readable format (available via the App's export feature)
  • Correction: The right to correct inaccurate data (editable directly within the App)

For users in the European Union or Switzerland, these rights are guaranteed under the GDPR and the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nFADP).

9. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as you use the App. Uninstalling the App or clearing its data will permanently delete all locally stored information. We do not retain copies of your data on our servers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
